What a beautiful Jasmine flower

"好一朵美丽的茉莉花(hao yi duo mei li de mo li hua)"

means "What a beautiful jasmine flower!"

This is a beautiful folk song of Jiangsu, China. This song adopts the traditional Chinese pentatonic mode, has distinct national characteristics, and is widely spread in the West. This song shows the euphemistic and soft Chinese Jiangnan style. It sings of an elegant girl attracted by the fragrant jasmine, unwilling to pluck it, unwilling to give it up. The songs are graceful and beautiful, and the feelings are deep and implicit.
